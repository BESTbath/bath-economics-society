## Reasons to Join Us

### BEST Socials 

* Meet like-minded individuals and potential coursemates at Economics Society socials! 
* Look out for society events both in the city and on campus
* Formal events with Spring Ball planned for 2026

### BEST Academic Events

Receive free weekly briefings of summarised Economics and Finance related news emailed to you every Monday by entering your email [here](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSfxI3Tz0zZrEVRMjlbj4H1yKfsFDvwyv4mEzpwoRs1p7iEgeA/viewform?usp=sf_link). 

Hear from some of the UK’s top Economists, Businesspeople & Professionals at:

* Panel Discussions
* Series of Guest Lectures

Also hoping to send a team to next year's Warwick Economics Summit!

Meet & network with some of the UK’s top graduate employers, including PwC and others, and attend panel discussions to get a greater insight into firms and organisations!
